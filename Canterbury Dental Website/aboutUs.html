About Canterbury Dental

  • Your Comfort Matters to Us

    We provide the highest quality of dental care available anywhere. We listen to our patients and treat your needs individually. Our office is clean and comfortable. We make every effort to be on time. We know your time is as valuable as ours. Canterbury Dental will strive to make every visit as efficient as possible. We will treat you with respect. Our tender and caring approach will make you comfortable and at ease. You can expect honest and customized treatment plans that are clearly explained to you. Our practice provides a full range of dental treatments including preventive dentistry, restorative dentistry, cosmetic dentistry, endodontics, root canal therapy, periodontics, Gingivitis teatment and more.

  • Dental Health is Physical Health

    Our team of health care professionals believes that healthy gums, fresh breath, and strong teeth will brighten your smile. And a beautiful smile is good for your emotional and physical health. Once upon a time, dental care was avoiding a toothache. But today we know that maintaining oral health is part of maintaining overall health. It reflects how you feel about yourself and affects how others view you. Your smile is the first impression you make as you meet someone new. And there are no second chances at a first impression.
